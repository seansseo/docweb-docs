# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date**: January 22, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of DocWeb. Violations may result in account suspension or termination.

## Responsible Crawling

### Respect robots.txt
DocWeb strictly follows robots.txt directives. You should not:
- Request discovery of sites that explicitly disallow our bot
- Attempt to circumvent crawling restrictions

### Rate Limiting
DocWeb has built-in rate limiting. You should not:
- Make excessive repeated discoveries of the same domain
- Attempt to bypass rate limits
- Deliberately overload target servers

### Crawl-Delay Compliance
We honor crawl-delay directives. Do not expect faster crawling than the target site allows.

## Permitted Use Cases

### Documentation Analysis
- Exploring technical documentation for learning
- Understanding API structures and capabilities
- Competitive intelligence on publicly available docs

### Research
- Academic research on information architecture
- SEO analysis of documentation sites
- User experience research

### Business Use
- Onboarding developers to new technologies
- Creating internal knowledge bases
- Training materials development

## Prohibited Activities

### Illegal Activities
- Accessing systems without authorization
- Violating computer fraud laws
- Infringing intellectual property

### Harmful Use
- Scraping personal or sensitive information
- Building datasets for malicious AI training
- Competitive harm to scraped sites

### Circumvention
- Bypassing authentication or paywalls
- Evading anti-bot measures
- Manipulating our credit system

### Abuse
- Creating multiple accounts to evade limits
- Automated abuse of our API
- Denial of service attacks

### Content Restrictions
Do not use DocWeb to discover or scrape:
- Personal data (unless authorized)
- Financial information
- Medical records
- Authentication credentials
- Proprietary source code
- Illegal content

## Third-Party Rights

### Intellectual Property
- Scraped content belongs to original owners
- Respect copyright and trademark laws
- Do not redistribute content without rights

### Terms of Service
- You are responsible for compliance with target site ToS
- Some sites prohibit automated access
- Obtain permission when required

## Reporting Violations

- Contact us at [seanseo.llc@gmail.com](mailto:seanseo.llc@gmail.com)

## Enforcement

### Violation Response
Depending on severity, we may:
1. Issue a warning
2. Temporarily suspend your account
3. Permanently terminate your account
4. Report to law enforcement

### Appeals
If you believe enforcement was in error:
- Contact us within 30 days
- Provide explanation and evidence
- We will review and respond
