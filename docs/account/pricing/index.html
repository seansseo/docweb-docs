FAQ​

How do credits work?​

Each discovery or chat message costs 1 credit. Credits reset daily at midnight UTC. See Credits System for details.

Can I change plans?​

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade anytime. See Managing Your Subscription.

What happens if I run out of credits?​

You'll need to wait until midnight UTC for credits to reset, or upgrade to a higher tier.

Is there a free trial for paid tiers?​

Currently, we offer the Free tier for unlimited trial. You can upgrade when ready.

Do unused credits roll over?​

No, credits reset daily and do not accumulate.

Can I cancel anytime?​

Yes, subscriptions can be canceled anytime via Stripe customer portal.

What payment methods are accepted?​

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe.
